        DONKEY.BAS
        ==========

The original version of "Donkey-kong" for the Sharp PC-1500 was published in
"Hebdogiciel" #86. This one was taken from http://www.pc1500.com and ported 
for the use with the PC-1403(H).

Start:
With DEF K or RUN

Keys:
4 - left
6 - right
8 - up
5 - jump 

Game: 
Jump over the barrels and climb the 10 levels... 
Up there, beware of the gorilla!

Sound           : **
